Cassandra Mobley | Product Designer

Hi, my name is Cassandra and I'm a multi-disciplined individual specializing in Product Development. My whole world is centered around a love for product design in digital and physical form. My passion allows me to delve deep into each process of the design to solve problems. Even though I've had a focus education wise in manufacturing I've always dreamed of designing and integrating products. This passion had led me to develop a multi-technology-based platform that teaches kids financial literacy through gamification with a line of books that tie into the topics for a younger audience. This curiousness has also led to learning basic welding to create physical storage solutions and other prototypes focused on children-based products.

Inspirational Designers

James Dyson

James Dyson innovated the way we cleaned with a new bagless vacuum cleaner, a new form of blowdryer and bladeless fans. Even though these objects are for pure function, he innovated a product with beautiful design exection.

Scandinavian Design

Scandinavian Design is a minimaliatic, clean design that pairs well with my OCD and anxiety quite well. In the design there are a multitude of textiles, shapes and simple lines. One of the main components in this design form is a lack of clutter and natural elements.

Jony Ive

As probably noticed above, I am a huge component of simple, beautiful and functional design which encompasses John Ive. He lead designs for Apple products such as the iPad, iPhone, and iPod which has impacted Apple's design philosophy to this day
